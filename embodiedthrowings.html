Embodied Throwings, 2018
This is a serie of bodies wich I have trown on the pottery wheel. Throwing is a very sensual given.
While they were still wet but not to wet, I transformed them with my own body. I only used my body to make them more physical.

By transforming the clay bodies I learnt my own body a lot better, because of the prints they left in the clay. Round, angular, flat. I pushed, squeezed, rubbeb the clay from the inside to the outside and vica versa. During this proces I learnt that I like to work with bigger pieces, because I can use my full body, it is more psyical.

This serie also reflects my red thread in my work. The most important thing for is a one on one relationship with the medium. In that way the artist and the medium have a realy close communication. That communication makes sure that I feel how the medium reacts on my handlings. It is important to explore, break trough and respect the fysical abbilities of the medium
