BIOGRAPHY

MY NAME IS TESSA LECLUYSE, I'M A BACHELOR STUDENT AT LUCA SCHOOL OF ARTS IN GENT.
THROWING POTTERY IS A PASSION WHICH I LOVE BECAUSE OF THE DIRECT CONTACT WITH THE MEDIUM. THIS I CAN SAY IS THE RED LINE IN MY WORK. I FIND IT VERY IMPORTANT TO HAVE A ONE ON ONE RELATION WITH THE MEDIUM. IN THIS WAY I CAN FEEL AND REGISTRATE HOW THE MEDIUM REACTS ON MY HANDLINGS. I NEED TO LISTEN TO THE MEDIUM IN WHAT HE IS CABABLE OF AND WHICH HE IS NOT. 

BESIDES POTTERY, I WORK WITH GLASS TOO. I MOSTLY BLOW GLASS IN FAVOR OF MY CERAMIC WORK;  I BLOW GLASS IN THIS WAY I WANT TO AMPLIFY CERTAIN FACETS OF THE MEDIUM BOTH CERAMIC AND GLASS; DEPENDING ON THE THEME OF MY WORK.

MOST OF MY ARTISTIC WORK IS NOT FUNCTIONAL, BUT I DO LOVE TO THROW AND BLOW FUNCTIONAL OBJECTS.
