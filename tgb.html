TRANSFORMED GLASS BODIES
This serie of glass bodies is a simular project as "Embodied Trowings" but this time I worked as the title tells with glass, mouth blown glass. I throwed several ceramic cilinders on the pottery wheel. While they were still very wet we used them as a mold for the glass. While my assistant blowed glass into the ceramic mold I transformed them with my hands which was possible because the clay was very soft and wet.
I pushed, pull, kicked the clay while there was a counterpressure of the glassblower. In that way the glass took form by getting altered aswell blown out.
